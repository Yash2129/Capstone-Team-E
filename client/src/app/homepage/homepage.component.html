About Us



At our core, we are dedicated to revolutionizing healthcare equipment management.
Our platform streamlines the intricate processes involved in tracking, maintaining,
and distributing medical equipment within healthcare facilities. By connecting hospitals,
maintenance technicians, and suppliers, we ensure seamless collaboration for efficient
healthcare delivery.

Comprehensive Management

Our platform enables hospitals to effectively manage their equipment inventory, schedule maintenance tasks, and submit equipment requests hassle-free.

Efficient Maintenance

Maintenance technicians receive real-time updates on tasks, enabling them to promptly address maintenance needs and manage parts inventory efficiently.

Streamlined Procurement

Suppliers gain insights into equipment demands, efficiently process orders, and manage shipments, ensuring timely delivery to healthcare facilities.
